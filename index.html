What is "the special" in Xianze!

We are here to entertain you!

Xianze is back as XIANZE EVOLVED which witnesses the tons of zest, spontaneity and innovation. Xianze is the annual techno fest of Mindbenders association in KG College of Arts and Science, Coimbatore. It is aimed at stimulating the youth to elevate aspirations, fight challenges and to achieve big. The objective is to showcase the dynamism of the youth today by providing them a platform to perform.

About Us

Mindbenders is a techno association holding the students from M.Sc Software Systems, M.Sc Computer Science and B.Sc Electronics and Communication System since 2011. The association has always been the pioneer in quelling challenges of the chosen fields through Research, Lectures, Self-Study, peers and faculty. MINDBENDERS organizes the technical symposium XIANZE every year.
